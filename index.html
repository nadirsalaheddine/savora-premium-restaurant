Our Story

Built by people who obsess over the small things.

Savora began as a single counter and a wood-fired oven. Today it's a full kitchen and café — same obsession with sourcing, same restraint on the plate. Every dish still passes through one hand before it reaches yours.

Seasonal Sourcing Chef-Led Kitchen Zero-Waste Prep
2016
A counter, a wood oven, one idea
Savora opens as a 12-seat counter focused on live-fire cooking and honest ingredients.
2019
The kitchen grows
A full dining room and pastry program join the original menu, led by Chef Elena Marchetti.
2022
Café & roastery added
Savora Café opens next door, sourcing single-origin beans roasted in-house weekly.
2026
Savora, online
Order, reserve, and explore the full menu — now available wherever you are.
